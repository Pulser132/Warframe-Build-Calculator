# Ability Attributes — Scaling & Caps

**Source:** Stage 4 Plan (decision 5: all four attributes + efficiency cap); wiki mechanics (to verify exact caps for other attributes)

## The four ability attributes

All attributes are modified **additively** by mods (each mod adds a percentage to the base 100%):
- Example: 100% base + 30% Intensify + 55% Blind Rage = `(1.00 + 0.30 + 0.55) × 100% = 185% Ability Strength`

### Ability Strength

- **Base:** 100%
- **Mods:** Intensify (+30%), Transient Fortitude (+55%), Blind Rage (+99%), Overextended (-60%), Power Drift (+15%)
- **Cap:** No documented cap (in-game, strength can reach very high values)
- **Affects:** ability damage, buff magnitudes (e.g., Roar: `+50% × strength`)

### Ability Duration

- **Base:** 100%
- **Mods:** Continuity (+30%), Transient Fortitude (-27.5%), Narrow Minded (+99%), Fleeting Expertise (-60%)
- **Cap:** No documented cap
- **Affects:** how long abilities remain active

### Ability Range

- **Base:** 100%
- **Mods:** Stretch (+45%), Overextended (+90%), Cunning Drift (+15%), Narrow Minded (-66%)
- **Cap:** No documented cap
- **Affects:** ability radius / area of effect

### Ability Efficiency

- **Base:** 100%
- **Mods:** Streamline (+30%), Blind Rage (-55%), Fleeting Expertise (+60%)
- **Cap:** **175%** (verified in Stage 4 Plan decision 5)
  - Efficiency exceeding 175% provides no additional benefit
  - Example: 100% base + 60% Fleeting + 30% Streamline = 190% → clamped to 175%
