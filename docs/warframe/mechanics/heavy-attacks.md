# Heavy Attack Mechanics

**Source**: https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Heavy_Attack and https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Melee (2026-06-17)

## Combo Consumption

- Heavy attacks consume **100% of your Melee Combo Count by default**
- Heavy Attack Efficiency mods reduce consumption without affecting damage multipliers
- Efficiency caps at 90% reduction, meaning minimum 10% consumption even fully stacked

## Damage Multiplier Formula

**Damage = Weapon Damage × Base Multiplier × Combo Multiplier**

Where:
- **Base Multiplier**: Fixed per weapon class (typically 5x-6x, varies by weapon)
- **Combo Multiplier**: Ranges from 2x to 12x based on current combo counter tier

Example: Staff with 400 modded attack damage at 3x combo multiplier = 400 × 5 × 3 = 6,000 damage

## Combo Multiplier Application

Heavy attacks scale with your current combo multiplier tier (2x at minimum combo, 12x at maximum 220+ hit count).

## Wind-Up Time & Attack Speed

- Each weapon has an inherent **wind-up duration** (ranges 0.4s to 1.2s depending on weapon type)
- **Critical point**: Increasing melee attack speed does **not** reduce wind-up time
- Attack speed instead **reduces the interval between heavy attacks** (time between wind-up completion and next heavy attack)

## Heavy Attack Efficiency Stat

Mods like Heavy Attack Efficiency reduce the percentage of combo counter consumed, allowing multiple heavy attacks before depletion. Does not affect damage multipliers—only consumption rate.
