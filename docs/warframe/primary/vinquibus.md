## Overview

**Type/Category:** Primary (Rifle)  
**Weapon Class:** Rifle with integrated bayonet melee form  
**Unique Trait:** Dual-slot weapon — occupies both primary and melee weapon slots simultaneously. Both forms rank together as a single mastery weapon.  
**Obtained:** Blueprint and components (Barrel, Blade, Receiver, Stock) drop from Roathe missions (12.5% chance each, Uncommon rarity)  
**Mastery Requirement:** 14  
**Build Cost:** 15,000 Credits (build time 12 hours)

## Special Mechanics

### Primary Form — Precision Buff System
- **Vinquibus Precision Buff:** Headshots grant 2 stacks (max 16), increasing melee damage by 250%
- Multishot and punch-through can generate additional stacks
- Stacks consumed on melee attack connection

### Melee Form — Ferocity Buff System
- **Vinquibus Ferocity Buff:** Kills grant 4 stacks (max 16), increasing primary ammo efficiency by 100%
- Stacks deplete on every shot regardless of hit success

### Melee Form Attacks
- **Standard Melee Strikes:** Normal bayonet attacks
- **Gunshot:** Silent projectile attack with forced Impact procs
- **Gunshot Explosion:** Area-of-effect blast dealing 400 blast damage with falloff
- **Slam Attacks:** Ground-based AoE with different damage types and ranges
- **Heavy Slam Attacks:** Enhanced ground-based AoE variant

### Modding System
- Primary and melee forms are modded separately
- Uses rifle mods for primary form, melee mods for melee form
- Separate riven mods generated for each form

## Technical Notes
- Cannot be used in melee-only missions
- Has restrictions in certain endgame activities (e.g., Deep Archimedea)
