## Incarnon Genesis Evolutions

Torid has 4 evolution tiers with selectable perks at each tier.

### EVO 1: Incarnon Form
Automatically unlocked when forma installed. Switches weapon to long-range Toxin beam.

### EVO 2: Choose One
- **Final Fusillade:** +51 base damage; final shot adds +3 Multishot
- **Plentiful Mayhem:** +31 base damage; multishot consumes ammo from reserves and boosts damage by +60%

### EVO 3: Choose One
- **Swift Deliverance:** +50% projectile speed
- **Renewed Horror:** On reload from empty, lingering damage field duration doubles on first shot
- **Extended Volley:** +9 magazine capacity

### EVO 4: Choose One
- **Commodore's Fortune:** +20% critical chance
- **Survivor's Edge:** +15% critical chance and +15% status chance
- **Elemental Balance:** +34% status chance
